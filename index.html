Welcome to the Book Of Liberty.

The Book of Liberty is a collection of essays written by anons who follow political (or otherwise) philosphies broadly classifiable as (for lack of a better word) Libertarian. This includes Anarcho-Capitalists, Capitalist Minarchists, Objectivists, and some Monarchists.

Because the essays span across different ideologies, you should not correlate them unless they belong to the same category.

If someone happens to possess a pdf of The Book of /liberty/, please comment!
